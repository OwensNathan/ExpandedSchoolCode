Northgard

Summary

Northgard is a real-time strategy (RTS) game developed by Shiro Games and released in 2017. Set in a Norse mythology-inspired world, the game tasks players with managing a Viking clan as they explore, settle, and conquer the harsh and unpredictable lands of Northgard. The game blends strategic base-building, resource management, and combat, with a focus on survival in an unforgiving environment.

Key Features

  • Setting: The game is set in a newly discovered continent called Northgard, a land of vast forests, dangerous creatures, and harsh winters. The world is inspired by Viking lore, and each clan represents a different aspect of Viking culture or mythology.
  • Clans: Players can choose from different clans, each with its own unique abilities and playstyle. Some clans focus on military strength, while others excel at resource gathering or diplomacy. Examples include the Clan of the Wolf, known for their warrior abilities, and the Clan of the Bear, which has better resource production and resilience.
  • Gameplay: The game revolves around exploration, resource management, and survival. Players must gather resources like food, wood, and stone, build structures, and expand their territory while facing challenges such as winter, wild animals, and rival clans. Combat involves sending units to fight for territory, and tactical decisions are crucial to victory.
  • Winter Mechanic: One unique feature of Northgard is the harsh winter cycle, which can drastically affect resource production. Players must plan ahead to survive through these cold months by managing food, resources, and morale to avoid starvation and unrest.
  • Victory Conditions: There are multiple ways to win the game, including military conquest, achieving specific objectives, and winning through dominance or influence over the land.

    • Story

    The game’s story follows a group of Vikings who set sail to explore and settle in the newly discovered land of Northgard after the death of their king. The player's clan must navigate through dangers like wild beasts, hostile tribes, and even mythical creatures. As the story unfolds, the Vikings uncover the mysterious forces behind the land's challenges.

    Multiplayer

    In addition to the single-player campaign, Northgard offers multiplayer mode, where players can compete against each other in a race for dominance over the land, managing resources, and defending against attacks from other clans.

    Detailed Clan Information

    Major Clans

  • Clan of the Wolf (Fenrir): Bonus: +15% Military unit production speed. Strong early game aggression with warrior focus.
  • Clan of the Stag (Eikthyrnir): Bonus: +5 Fame per trade route. Excels at economic and diplomatic victories through trading.
  • Clan of the Raven (Huginn and Muninn): Bonus: Can colonize with Kröwns instead of food. Masters of mercantile strategies and harbor income.
  • Clan of the Bear (Bjarki): Bonus: +15% to all production. Best for defensive play with strong units and resilience.
  • Clan of the Boar (Slidrugtanni): Bonus: Lore boost, -20% Food consumption. Food-focused clan excellent for Wisdom victories.
  • Clan of the Snake (Sváfnir): Bonus: Can use Kröwns to recruit Skalds. Unique strategic options with Skalds providing various benefits.
  • Clan of the Dragon (Nidhogg): Bonus: +2 Happiness per zone. Plays differently without Happiness building requirements.

    • Advanced Gameplay Mechanics

    Territory Control

    Unlike traditional RTS games, Northgard divides the map into distinct zones that must be colonized. Each zone can only support a limited number of buildings, forcing strategic decisions about resource allocation. Players must balance military zones for defense, food production zones for sustenance, and resource zones for economic growth.

    Fame System

    Fame is earned through various achievements like defeating enemies, completing objectives, or trading. Higher fame levels provide bonuses and are essential for certain victory conditions. Players compete for fame milestones that grant powerful advantages.

    Lore and Technologies

    The Lore system allows players to research unique technologies that improve their civilization. Each clan has access to special Lore that enhances their unique playstyle. Key Lore categories include:

  • Military Lore: Unlocks better military units and improvements.
  • Economic Lore: Boosts resource production and trade efficiency.
  • Defense Lore: Improves fortifications and unit defense.

    • Victory Conditions

  • Conquest: Capture the enemy Town Hall and hold it for a period.
  • Wisdom: Accumulate 500 Lore points (1,500 in multiplayer).
  • Trade: Build a Trading Post and accumulate 4,000 Kröwns to buy the trade victory.
  • Fame: Be the first to reach 1,000 fame (500 in some game modes).
  • Map-Specific: Some maps have unique objectives like controlling all Yggdrasil fragments.

    • Legacy

    Northgard has been praised for its depth and strategic gameplay, combining classic RTS elements with survival mechanics and a unique Norse setting. It stands out in the RTS genre with its focus on resource management, survival tactics, and the dynamic challenge of seasonal changes. The game has received numerous expansions and DLC adding new clans, each with unique mechanics that dramatically change gameplay strategies.
