Summary

Northgard is a real-time strategy (RTS) game developed by Shiro Games and released in 2017. Set in a Norse mythology-inspired world, the game tasks players with managing a Viking clan as they explore, settle, and conquer the harsh and unpredictable lands of Northgard. The game blends strategic base-building, resource management, and combat, with a focus on survival in an unforgiving environment.

Key Features

  • Setting: The game is set in a newly discovered continent called Northgard, a land of vast forests, dangerous creatures, and harsh winters. The world is inspired by Viking lore, and each clan represents a different aspect of Viking culture or mythology.
  • Clans: Players can choose from different clans, each with its own unique abilities and playstyle. Some clans focus on military strength, while others excel at resource gathering or diplomacy. Examples include the Clan of the Wolf, known for their warrior abilities, and the Clan of the Bear, which has better resource production and resilience.
  • Gameplay: The game revolves around exploration, resource management, and survival. Players must gather resources like food, wood, and stone, build structures, and expand their territory while facing challenges such as winter, wild animals, and rival clans. Combat involves sending units to fight for territory, and tactical decisions are crucial to victory.
  • Winter Mechanic: One unique feature of Northgard is the harsh winter cycle, which can drastically affect resource production. Players must plan ahead to survive through these cold months by managing food, resources, and morale to avoid starvation and unrest.
  • Victory Conditions: There are multiple ways to win the game, including military conquest, achieving specific objectives, and winning through dominance or influence over the land.

    Multiplayer

    In addition to the single-player campaign, Northgard offers multiplayer mode, where players can compete against each other in a race for dominance over the land, managing resources, and defending against attacks from other clans.

    Legacy

    Northgard has been praised for its depth and strategic gameplay, combining classic RTS elements with survival mechanics and a unique Norse setting. It stands out in the RTS genre with its focus on resource management, survival tactics, and the dynamic challenge of seasonal changes.
